Thank you for your interest in hosting your challenge on EvalAI. We're excited to support your work.

Each plan includes one always-on worker to handle submissions (except for the Remote Evaluation Plan, which relies on your own infrastructure). If you expect higher submission volume—especially near deadlines—we can scale easily by adding additional workers. These extra workers will be billed separately according to your selected plan.
