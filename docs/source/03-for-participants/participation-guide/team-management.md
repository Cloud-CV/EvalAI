# Team Management

Participants compete as a **participant team**. A team can have one or more members sharing submissions and leaderboard entries.

## Participant team basics

- Each challenge participation is tied to one participant team.
- Team name is unique per challenge context.
- Invite collaborators from your EvalAI account settings and team UI on the challenge page.

## Creating a team

During first-time participation, EvalAI prompts you to create a team or select an existing one. See [Getting Started](getting-started.html).

## Collaborating

Add teammates who will upload submissions or manage results. Ensure everyone understands:

- Submission limits (`max_submissions_per_day`, etc.) apply per team
- Which submission is selected for the public leaderboard when `is_restricted_to_select_one_submission` is enabled

## Host vs participant teams

Do not confuse **participant teams** (competitors) with **host teams** (organizers). Host teams manage challenge configuration; participant teams only submit and appear on leaderboards.
