# Privacy Policy

EvalAI's hosted service at [eval.ai](https://eval.ai) is operated by the CloudCV team. For the authoritative privacy policy applicable to the production website, refer to the legal pages linked from the EvalAI footer or contact [team@eval.ai](mailto:team@eval.ai).

## Self-hosted deployments

If you operate a fork of EvalAI, you are responsible for publishing your own privacy policy and data handling practices for your users.

## Data typically processed

- Account information (email, profile)
- Submissions and uploaded files
- Leaderboard and team metadata
- Server logs for security and debugging
