# Team Collaboration Example

Multiple researchers can share one **participant team** on a challenge.

## Setup

1. One member creates the team during [participation](../../03-for-participants/participation-guide/getting-started.html).
2. Invite collaborators via EvalAI team management (UI).
3. Agree internally on who submits to avoid exhausting `max_submissions_per_day`.

## Single leaderboard submission

When the host sets `is_restricted_to_select_one_submission: true`, only one submission can be highlighted on the public leaderboard. Coordinate which run to expose.

## Private vs public

Teams may keep submissions private until review is complete, then toggle **Show on Leaderboard** per submission when allowed.
